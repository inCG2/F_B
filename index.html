Football Definition

Soccer is a ball sport in which the goal is to score points and win or lose by putting a soccer ball into the opponent's goal,
mainly using the feet, without touching the ball with the hands and arms.

Refrain from touching the ball with your hands and arms, and use the goalpost.
These two are the essence and core of football.

Association Football Positions

The location of 11 players from each team on the soccer field where they are deployed to perform team tactics.
Each team consists of one goalkeeper and 10 outfield players, who are divided into defenders,
midfielders and strikers according to the formation.
